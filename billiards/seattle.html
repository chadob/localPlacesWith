About LOCAL PLACES WITH
What is Local Places With?

Local Places With is a map of places with fun amenities like bar sports, live entertainment, and games.

How does Local Places With define the amenities?
  • Bar Sports: Most likely found in a bar. Doesn’t require electricity. And a little athletic skill never hurts.
  • Live Entertainment: Social and musical activities geared toward an audience or group participation.
  • Games: Not quite a sport, not quite a social event, but probably just as fun.

Who finds all these local places?

You do! Feel free to add or update one here.

Our STORY
The Inspiration for Local Places With

The idea for Local Places With came to us back in 2013 when a couple of us were out and about in downtown Seattle. We wanted to play pool but realized none of us actually knew of any places to play pool nearby. Eventually, we started looking for local places with billiards more often and started keeping track of a collection of bars with pool tables and other bar games. One thing lead to another, things like karaoke and trivia nights and arcades bars started showing up on our lists, until finally one of us slapped a sloppily-designed Powerpoint presentation in front of everyone’s faces, and we came to the conclusion that Local Places With had to happen.

How Local Places With is different from other websites

While we love using tools like Yelp and Google, we want to create a website that focuses on finding only specific amenities or activities for users, such as bars with pool tables or other bar games, rather than attempting to provide every possible piece of information about a place. We’ll leave that task to veterans of the industry like Yelp and Google!

The Vision for Local Places With

Our goal for Local Places With is to create a website that helps users find the activities and amenities they love the most, while also being fun and easy-to-use. Currently, we’ve focused on locating bars with billiards and other bar games, but that will soon carry over into places with karaoke and dancing, trivia nights and arcades, and we really see it expanding into a ton of different areas!
